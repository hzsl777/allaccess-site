Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect a streaming service?

Go to Settings and tap the service you want to connect. You'll be redirected to log in with your account. AllAccess uses secure OAuth — we never see your password.

Can I use AllAccess without a subscription?

AllAccess works with free tiers where available (SoundCloud, YouTube). Premium features like high-quality streaming require an active subscription with the respective service.

How do I disconnect a service?

Go to Settings, find the connected service, and tap Disconnect. This removes your login tokens from the device.

Why aren't lyrics showing for a song?

Lyrics availability depends on the song and source. AllAccess checks Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, Genius, and embedded metadata. Some songs simply don't have lyrics available from any source.

How does P2P file transfer work?

Both devices need AllAccess installed and must be on the same local network or within Bluetooth range. Go to a song or playlist, tap Share, and choose "Send to Nearby."

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all local data. For CloudKit social data (profile, messages, blends), email us and we'll process the deletion.
