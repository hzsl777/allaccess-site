Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

AllAccess ("the App") is a music aggregation app that connects to third-party streaming services. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

  • Authentication tokens — OAuth tokens for connected services (Spotify, Tidal, YouTube, SoundCloud, Apple Music) are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. They are never sent to our servers.
  • User profile — If you use social features, your display name, username, and avatar are stored in Apple CloudKit and visible to your friends.
  • Music activity — Shared songs, playlists, blend data, and messages between friends are stored in Apple CloudKit.
  • Cached data — Lyrics and artwork are cached locally on your device for offline access and performance.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage telemetry.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not store your streaming service passwords.
  • We do not track your location.

Third-Party Services

AllAccess connects to the following services using their official APIs. Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • Spotify
  • Tidal
  • Google / YouTube
  • SoundCloud
  • Apple Music
  • Genius

CloudKit & iCloud

Social features use Apple CloudKit (Public Database). Data stored includes user profiles, friend relationships, messages, and shared playlists. This data is governed by Apple's iCloud terms and is associated with your Apple ID.

Local Storage

Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. Lyrics and artwork caches are stored in the app's Caches directory and may be cleared by the system at any time.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect any streaming service at any time from Settings, which deletes the associated tokens. Deleting the app removes all local data. To delete CloudKit data, contact us at the support page.

Children's Privacy

AllAccess is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact
